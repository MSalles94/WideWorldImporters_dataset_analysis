# Business Questions
<p> For better understanding the database we answer some business questions.</p>
<p>This is the questions summary. You may find links to the respective solutions.</p>


## [Level 1 — Basic](./BusinessQuestions_L1.md)

1. Who are the top 10 customers by number of orders placed?
2. What are the top 10 best-selling products by quantity sold?
3. What is the company's total revenue?
4. How many customers exist in each customer category?
5. How many products exist in each product category?
6. Which are the top 10 provinces with the highest number of registered customers?
7. What was the total revenue by item category?
8. What was the total revenue by month?
9. Which is the invoice per salespeople registered in 2015?
10. Which delivery methods are most frequently used by customers?

## [Level 2 — Intermediate](./BusinessQuestions_L2.md)
11. Which customers generated the highest revenue for the company?
12. Which products generated the highest total revenue?
13. What is the average order value per customer?
14. Which cities have the highest average revenue per customer?
15. What is the percentage contribution of each product category to total revenue?
16. Which customers have not made purchases during the last month available in the dataset?
17. Is there seasonality in sales? Which months show the highest and lowest revenue?
18. What is the average delivery lead time after picking completion, by delivery method?
19. Which salespeople have the highest average sales value per order?
20. Which combinations of city, and product category generate the highest revenue?


## [Level 3 — Advanced](./BusinessQuestions_L3.md)


21. Which customers show the highest revenue growth between consecutive years?
22. How concentrated is company revenue? What percentage of total revenue is generated by the top 20% of customers?
23. Which products demonstrate consistent sales growth over time?
24. Which customers exhibit recurring and predictable purchasing behavior?
25. What is the average repurchase cycle per customer?
26. Are there customers with a high number of orders but low average revenue per order? Who are they?
27. Which product categories have the highest growth potential based on historical sales performance?
28. What is the impact of each salesperson on total revenue over time?
29. Which regions have the greatest expansion potential considering customer count and average revenue?
30. How can customers be segmented into high, medium, and low-value groups based on revenue and purchase frequency?

## [Level 4 — Data Warehouse & Dashboard Design](./BusinessQuestions_L4.md)


31. Which KPIs should be included in an executive sales dashboard?
32. How should a sales fact table be designed, and which dimensions are required?
33. Which metrics should be calculated in the Data Warehouse layer instead of the dashboard layer?
34. Which indicators can be used to monitor customer retention over time?
35. How should salesperson performance be measured considering order volume, revenue, and average order value?
36. Which products should be classified as strategic products for the business?
37. How can VIP customers be identified using revenue, frequency, and recency criteria?
38. Which indicators help monitor logistics and delivery efficiency?
39. Which dimensions are most relevant for explaining revenue fluctuations?
40. How can an executive view be designed to quickly answer: "Who sold what, to whom, when, and where?" using a dimensional model?
